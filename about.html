Our Mission

The mission of SquadUp is to eliminate the frustration of what we gamers call "Solo Queuing" by connecting gamers through a compatibility-driven social network that prioritizes skill level, playstyle, and positive community interaction.

Our Core Values

  • Zero Toxicity: A strict reputation system to keep lobbies positive.
  • True Skill Matching: Finding teammates who actually match your rank and playstyle.
  • Cross-Platform Connections: Uniting PC and Console players, putting that battle to rest.
